# Leaderboard for the 2026 Challenge

The leaderboard shows the Challenge [score](../#scoring/) from the official phase of the Challenge on the hidden validation set. Age-conditioned area under the receiver operating characteristic curve (AUROC) determines the Challenge winners; the prevalence-based reward metric and other metrics reported by the Challenge scoring code provide additional context. We will release the [results](../results/) of the Challenge on the hidden test set after the end of the official phase.
